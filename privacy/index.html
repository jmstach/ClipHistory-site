ClipHistory

Privacy

The app

ClipHistory runs entirely on your Mac. Your clipboard history is stored encrypted on disk with AES‑256‑GCM; the key lives in your Keychain, never on disk. It does not create user accounts and has no telemetry. Clipboard data from password managers is skipped automatically, and you can exclude any app from being recorded.

The only network request the app makes is an update check on launch, which asks GitHub whether a newer version exists. Your clipboard contents never leave your machine.

The website

If you enter your email address to be notified about updates, that address is sent to this site and used only to email you about ClipHistory. It is never sold or shared. There is no other tracking on this site at present; should privacy‑friendly, cookieless analytics be added later, this page will be updated to say so.
